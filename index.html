Adding a recipe

Open data/recipes.json and add a new recipe. The recipe can be a URL, text, or a file. 

{
  "title": "Recipe name",
  "category": "Chicken",
  "tags": ["easy", "air fryer"],
  "url": "https://example.com/recipe"
}
